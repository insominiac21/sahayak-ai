# Scheme 8 — Stand-Up India Scheme

## Overview
Stand-Up India is a Government of India scheme to facilitate **bank loans from ₹10 lakh to ₹1 crore** to:
- **at least one SC/ST borrower**, and
- **at least one woman borrower**
**per bank branch**, for setting up a **greenfield enterprise** in **manufacturing, services, trading**, and (as updated in portal text) **agri-allied activities**.

## Eligibility
- State:
  - Pan-India (covers branches of Scheduled Commercial Banks; actual processing happens at the bank branch / LDM route).
- Age:
  - Not specified as a fixed “scheme age” in the scheme guidelines page; borrower must be legally eligible to contract/borrow as per bank rules.
- Income:
  - No fixed income slab; eligibility is category-based (SC/ST/Woman) + project viability + bank appraisal.
- Category:
  - **SC** or **ST** entrepreneur OR **Woman** entrepreneur.
  - For **non-individual enterprises (company/LLP/partnership)**: **at least 51% shareholding and controlling stake** must be held by **SC/ST and/or Woman entrepreneur**.
  - Enterprise must be **greenfield** (first-time venture in the proposed business line).
- Occupation:
  - Not required; the scheme is entrepreneurship-focused.

## Checklist
(Banks may ask for additional documents based on the project and local policy. This is the common baseline.)
- Identity + address proof (KYC) as per bank norms (Aadhaar/PAN etc.)
- Category proof (SC/ST certificate **or** woman entrepreneur details; as applicable)
- Business plan / DPR (project report) including cost, revenue, working capital need
- Proof of business setup readiness:
  - proposed location / rent/lease/ownership proof (if available)
  - required registrations/licenses (as applicable to the sector)
- Bank account details and recent statements (for appraisal / tracking)
- Quotes/proforma invoices for machinery/equipment (if purchasing)
- Any margin money support documents (if using State/Central schemes)
- Undertakings/declarations as required by the bank (e.g., greenfield status)

## Sections
- Objective & target group (SC/ST + Women, greenfield enterprises)
- Nature of loan
  - Composite loan (term loan + working capital)
  - Typical coverage: up to ~75% of project cost (bank appraisal based)
- Pricing & repayment (as per scheme guideline)
  - Interest rate linked to bank MCLR/base rate + spread (cap described in guideline)
  - Repayment up to ~7 years with moratorium up to ~18 months (as per guideline)
- Margin money & contribution
  - Margin money can be supported via convergence with other schemes
  - Borrower typically brings minimum own contribution (as per guideline)
- Credit guarantee / collateral
  - Collateral-free support via CGSSI route (where applicable)
- How to apply
  - Apply at bank branch OR through Stand-Up India portal OR via Lead District Manager (LDM)
  - Portal supports “handholding” (training, DPR prep, margin support, mentoring)
- Grievance redressal & tracking (portal-based tracking + bank contact points)
