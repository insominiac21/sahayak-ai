# Scheme 2 — PMJDY (Pradhan Mantri Jan-Dhan Yojana)

## Overview
PMJDY is India’s National Mission for Financial Inclusion. It enables an unbanked person/household to open a **basic savings bank deposit (BSBD) account** (typically **zero-balance**), and access services like **RuPay debit card**, **remittances**, **credit/overdraft (eligible cases)**, and **insurance/pension linkage** through a bank branch or Bank Mitra/Business Correspondent outlet.

## Eligibility
- State:
  - Pan-India (available through participating banks/Bank Mitra outlets).
- Age:
  - No fixed “scheme age” requirement to open an account (bank KYC rules apply).
  - Overdraft facility is for eligible account holders and has an age band (see notes under sections).
  - Minors: a minor **above 10 years** can open a savings account in a bank (bank rules apply).
- Income:
  - No income slab requirement mentioned for opening a PMJDY account.
- Category:
  - Primarily intended for **unbanked persons/households** (those without another bank account).
- Occupation:
  - No occupation requirement.

## Checklist
(Exact KYC/fields depend on the bank. Keep this as your “minimum expected set” for the assistant.)
- Proof of identity + address as per bank KYC (bring Aadhaar if available, otherwise bank-accepted KYC documents)
- Passport-size photo (if asked by the bank)
- Mobile number (for SMS alerts / seeding where required)
- Nominee details (recommended; bank will guide)

## Sections
- What you get (features/benefits):
  - Zero-balance BSBD account (no minimum balance requirement)
  - RuPay debit card
  - Accident insurance cover linked to RuPay card (amount depends on account opening date / conditions)
- Overdraft (OD) / credit access:
  - OD can be considered after **satisfactory operation for ~6 months**
  - OD limit updates and eligibility (age band, conditions) as per continuation guidelines
- How to open / where:
  - Bank branch or Business Correspondent (Bank Mitra) outlet
- DBT linkage / JAM pipeline:
  - Used as a channel to receive government benefits through DBT where applicable
- FAQs / common problems:
  - dormant/inactive accounts, re-KYC, mobile seeding, RuPay usage
