# Scheme 6 — Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana (SSY) / Sukanya Samriddhi Account (SSA)

## Overview
SSY (Sukanya Samriddhi Account) is a Government-backed small savings scheme to help parents/guardians build a dedicated fund for a **girl child’s education and future needs**. The account can be opened at **India Post offices** and **authorised bank branches**, is **transferable across India**, and offers tax benefits as per prevailing rules.

## Eligibility
- State:
  - Pan-India (Post Offices + notified bank branches; operational process may vary by branch/bank).
- Age:
  - Account can be opened **from birth until the girl child attains 10 years**.
  - Guardian operates/manages the account until the girl turns **18** (then the account holder can operate it after submitting required documents).
- Income:
  - No income slab requirement.
- Category:
  - For **Indian girl child** (account is opened in the girl’s name by parent/legal guardian).
  - Only **one account per girl child**.
  - A family can open accounts for **maximum two girl children**, with exceptions for **twins/triplets** (subject to affidavit + birth certificates).
- Occupation:
  - Not required.

## Checklist
(Branches may ask for additional KYC/verification.)
- Sukanya Samriddhi Account opening form (available at bank/post office)
- Birth certificate of the girl child
- Identity proof of parent/guardian (as per RBI KYC)
- Address/residence proof of parent/guardian (as per RBI KYC)
- Aadhaar number (commonly requested)
- PAN or Form 60 (as applicable)

## Sections
- Deposit rules
  - Minimum yearly deposit requirement (to keep account active)
  - Maximum deposit limit per financial year
  - Deposits are required only for the first **15 years** from account opening
- Interest (policy-controlled)
  - Interest rate is notified by Government from time to time; credited as per scheme rules
- Withdrawals for education
  - Partial withdrawal up to **50%** for education purposes (conditions apply)
- Maturity & early closure
  - Account matures after **21 years** from opening
  - Early closure allowed only in specific conditions (e.g., marriage after 18, death), with rules/limits
- Transfer & operations
  - Transferable across India
  - Guardian operates until the girl turns **18**
- Tax benefits
  - Deposits may qualify under **Section 80C** as per prevailing tax rules
