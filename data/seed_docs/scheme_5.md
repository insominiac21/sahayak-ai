# Scheme 5 — NSAP (National Social Assistance Programme)

## Overview
NSAP is a central programme that provides **social assistance** to vulnerable citizens through five components:
- IGNOAPS (Old Age Pension)
- IGNWPS (Widow Pension)
- IGNDPS (Disability Pension)
- NFBS (National Family Benefit Scheme – one-time assistance)
- Annapurna (food grains for eligible seniors not covered under old age pension)

## Eligibility
- State:
  - Pan-India (implemented by States/UTs; application + payout processes can vary by State/UT).
- Age:
  - **IGNOAPS:** 60+ (higher central assistance at 80+)  
  - **IGNWPS:** widow, 40–59  
  - **IGNDPS:** 18–59 (severe/multiple disabilities)  
  - **NFBS:** primary breadwinner death: 18–64  
  - **Annapurna:** senior citizens eligible for old age pension but **uncovered**
- Income:
  - **BPL household** requirement (as per Govt. of India / State BPL identification criteria).
- Category:
  - BPL + specific condition by component:
    - old age / widow / severe disability / bereaved household / uncovered eligible senior citizen
- Occupation:
  - Not required.

## Checklist
(Exact documents depend on State/UT workflow, but these are the common minimum.)
- Aadhaar (or State/UT accepted ID) + mobile number
- Proof of BPL status / ration card / State beneficiary list reference (as applicable)
- Bank/Post Office account details for DBT (passbook/cancelled cheque as asked)
- Age proof (for IGNOAPS)
- Widow proof + spouse death certificate (for IGNWPS)
- Disability certificate showing severe/multiple disability (for IGNDPS)
- Death certificate of primary breadwinner + local verification details (for NFBS)
- Residence details (often required by State/UT for verification)

## Sections
- Components & assistance amounts (central assistance)
  - IGNOAPS: ₹200 (60–79), ₹500 (80+)
  - IGNWPS: ₹300 (40–59)
  - IGNDPS: ₹300 (18–59, severe/multiple disabilities)
  - NFBS: ₹20,000 (one-time)
  - Annapurna: 10kg food grains/month (eligible seniors not covered under old age pension)
- How to apply (State/UT channels)
  - Online registration / application where enabled (portal/app)
  - Offline: local body/block/district/CSC routes (varies by State)
- Payments & tracking
  - DBT via PFMS/APB used in many States/UTs; disbursal periodicity varies
  - Track application/payment status on NSAP services where available
- Common issues & clarifications
  - “State top-up” amounts vary (central is baseline; States may add extra)
  - Migration between schemes by age (e.g., widow/disability pension age bands vs old-age pension)
