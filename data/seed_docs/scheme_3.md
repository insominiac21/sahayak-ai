# Scheme 3 — PMUY (Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana) / Ujjwala 2.0

## Overview
PMUY provides **LPG connections** to **adult women** from **poor households** to enable clean cooking fuel access. Under Ujjwala 2.0, there is a simplified eligibility approach using a **deprivation declaration**, with special provisions for **migrant households**.

## Eligibility
- State:
  - Pan-India via Oil Marketing Company (OMC) distributors (availability depends on service area/distributor coverage).
- Age:
  - Applicant must be a **woman aged 18 years or above**.
- Income:
  - Not defined as a fixed income slab; eligibility is based on being a **poor household** (deprivation/exclusion declaration as per prescribed format).
- Category:
  - Must belong to a **poor household** and submit the required declaration.
  - Additional eligibility routes exist via official beneficiary category lists (e.g., based on deprivation/identified poor household criteria).
- Occupation:
  - No occupation requirement.

## Checklist
(Exact fields may vary slightly by distributor/State; this is the “common minimum” mentioned in official docs.)
- PMUY KYC application form
- Family composition proof (e.g., ration card / state document certifying family composition) **OR** self-declaration (for migrant applicants)
- Aadhaar of beneficiary + adult family members listed in family composition document
- Proof of Address (Aadhaar alone may suffice if connection address matches Aadhaar; otherwise accepted POA documents as per KYC list)
- Bank account details (for any applicable transfers/verification)
- Any additional undertaking/declaration required by the distributor (e.g., deprivation declaration format for poor households)
